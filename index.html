Autumn Cozy Place

Three.js Journey Challenge — October 2025
Theme: “Cozy Place” (hosted by Bruno Simon)

Why I Made This

I created this scene to participate in the October 2025 Three.js Journey challenge. The theme “Cozy Place” inspired me to build a quiet autumn corner where warmth and stillness meet.

Concept

Autumn Cozy Place blends three things I love:

  • Autumn — my favorite season, full of calm light and crisp air.
  • Coffee — the ritual that anchors slow, reflective moments.
  • Ghibli-inspired vibes — gentle, hand-crafted atmosphere guiding the color, shapes, and composition.

I wanted a spot where you can enjoy outdoor autumn scenery and relax with a good coffee.

About Me

I’m Supachai Chanarittichai, a full-stack web developer currently exploring 3D rendering technologies like Blender and Three.js. My goal is to bring real-time 3D experiences into my future projects.
